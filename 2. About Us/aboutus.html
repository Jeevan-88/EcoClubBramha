ORG. PROFILE

GOAL

Organize, educate and enable the unorganized and neglected poor for overall development of themselves, nature, and the nation.

VISION

An Environmentally Healthy Nation with Social Justice and Economic Self-Reliance of the Deprived

MISSION

To facilitate a healthy environment and social justice for all the deprived.

STRATEGIES

  • Community Organization and Education
  • Organizing and caring for Persons With Disabilities
  • Seminars, Workshops, Demonstrations, and Trainings
  • Promoting Economic Self-Reliance of the deprived

Focused Stakeholders

Poorest of the Poor of various vulnerable communities, women, children, and Farmers irrespective of caste and creed.

Methodology

  • Participatory, Integrated, and Sustainable approach
  • Networking of common interest groups.
  • Field programs that serve as demonstrations, models, show cases, etc.
  • Communication through multimedia approaches and latest tools.
  • Public policy analysis through research, consultancy, debates, and campaigns.

Strengths

  • Trained experienced motivated and dedicated staff.
  • Good rapport at grassroots level with Local District Administration.
  • Access to experts, research institutions, and scientists.
  • Networking partnership of various VO's and CSO's.
  • The organization & Chief Functionary & Staff actively involved in many developmental activities, Seminars, Workshops, Rallies, etc.

AIMS & OBJECTIVES

  • To Aware the people towards better environment.
  • To promote the level of the Health & Sanitation in rural and urban areas.
  • To aware the rural farmers towards Sustainable Agriculture and Watershed Management.
  • To bring the Awareness on Climate Change.
  • To promote the Livestock & Diary Development in rural areas.
  • To enhance the livelihood opportunities in rural communities.
  • To establish the Village Knowledge Centers in villages.
  • To bring the awareness on Disability.
  • To rehabilitate and improve their qualities of lives of Persons With Mentally Challenged.
  • To promote Self Help movement among the Women, Youth, Disabled and Vulnerable section of the Society.
  • To promote the Resources and its developments with related Institutions.
